The idea

You do not need to invent a command center. tmux already keeps the session alive; impo.ai makes it reachable.

Why tmux?

tmux already has the hard part: sessions stay alive, windows keep their context, and panes can be reattached from somewhere else. Agents can run there without caring whether you are at your desk or on your phone.

impo.ai adds the missing network layer. A small connector runs next to tmux, dials out, and lets your browser drive the real pane.
